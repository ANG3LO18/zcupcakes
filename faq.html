Payment

An initial non-refundable deposit of 50% of the total cost is required to confirm and hold a reservation. An order is not considered active until payment is received.

We accept payments by Cash, Cash on Delivery or GCash. For your convenience you may pay your invoice using our Online Payment Option

A deposit of 50% is due at time of order placement. Balance is due in full 7 days prior to the event date for cake orders and 14 days prior for dessert table orders. We must receive the balance by 5 pm on the due date indicated in your contract to begin working on your order. Failure to do so will result in a cancelation of the order, contract will be null and void, and deposit will not be refunded.

The Balance due date is the final date for any cancelation or changes to the event date, size, style or flavors of the cake or desserts. All requests for cancellation must be made in writing. Cancellations received after the balance due date will not be subject to refund. In the event of an emergency, should we fail to deliver your cake or desserts on the date requested a refund for the full amount will be issued to you.
